My mission

My professional interests lie at the interface of technology, aviation, strategy, and sustainability. I have a broad technical, managerial, and research experience, having worked for airlines, academic institutes, and aerospace manufacturers. I have dealt with the technical and strategic problematics of the design, simulation, operation, and maintenance of aircraft systems and simulation of aviation processes, having led numerous projects and research initiatives. I serve on the Rulemaking Committee on Trustworthy AI (RMT.0742) for the European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) and I lead the Working Group on Diagnostics and Prognostics of the Independent Data Consortium for Aviation (IDCA), next to several other appointments on Technical Advisory Boards and international Working Groups.

  • Focus: Simulation & Digital Twins, Diagnostics & Prognostics, Autonomy, Trustworthy AI for Safety-critical Systems.
